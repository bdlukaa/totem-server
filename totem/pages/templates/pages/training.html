Totem offers a Keeper training program to those interested in better understanding themselves and those around them.

The Keeper training program is an essential part of Totem. Keepers make Circles go round!

Keeper training curriculum

Week 1

An introductory session lead by a Totem Keeper to familiarize you with all the details of Circle, such as the importance of Circles, our mission and values at Totem, the how to's of Circle, how to guide others through Circles.

Week 2

Participate in a Circle with your trainer as the Keeper. During this session you're encouraged to ask clarifying questions, take notes on Circle structure, be present, open and vulnerable.

Week 3-5

Attend two Circles lead by your fellow Keepers in training and lead one Circle of your own.

Keeper Training offer you a guide to mastering the art of personal story telling. Through this practice, you’ll learn how to be more present, open-minded, and learn how to release patterns of thoughts which no longer serve you.

You’ll learn how to write and design your own prompts for your Circles. Writing prompts in your own voice allows you to model vulnerability, fostering a sense of trust within your Circles.

Our training program will teach you how to create spaces in which Circle participants feel safe to share, be seen, and be heard.

The Totem Keeper Training program gives you a comprehensive curriculum and real life Circle experiences with other trained keepers.

The Keeper Training program costs $200. Once you complete the Keeper Training Program you'll have the confidence and knowledge to run your own Circles. You will also receive:

  • A certificate of completion
  • The Keeper handbook
  • Access to expert Keeper prompts
  • Access to Keeper's Only Circles
  • Access to Circles
  • Mentorship from your Keeper Trainer
